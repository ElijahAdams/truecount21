About

My name is Elijah and I created this app. It's a fun little pet project for me. Playing cards has always been an interest of mine from a young age. It was a fun pastime and a right of passage to be able to play certain games with aunts and uncles growing up. We bonded over games of Tonk, Gin Rummy, Spades, etc. I stumbled upon BlackJack in later years after my first trip to a North Carolina casino with my best friend. Until this day I still play at the 10 dollar table lol.

I'd attribute my love for cards as my gateway into computer science which is what enabled me to create this app. I enjoyed applying math to real topics but never knew of a way to turn that into a profession. I lucked up in college with a professor who knew just what I needed. In my freshman year of college, I took a math class. I don't recall the name but one of the topics in that class was counting cards because of the math principles it taught. Following those particular lessons, I asked the professor what would be the closest major to what he was teaching in this course. He said Computer Science. The next year I switched my major to Computer Science from Media Studies and never looked back.
